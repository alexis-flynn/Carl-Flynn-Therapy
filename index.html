About Me

I am an experienced Integrative & Cognitive-Behavioural Psychotherapist who uses various approaches (including person-centred, psychodynamic, CBT, Gestalt, and existential therapy) to meet clients' needs. I work with a number of issues, including anxiety, depression, relationship issues, shame, trauma, phobias, bullying, self-esteem, and many others. My aim is to work collaboratively with you to enable you to live a more fulfilling life.

FAQs

How long are sessions?

  • Sessions are typically 50 mins, although time frames can be adapted to meet individual and clinical needs - e.g., longer sessions for some PTSD sessions and shorter when reviewing graded exposure progress.

How frequent are sessions?

  • Sessions are typically weekly or fortnightly to maintain therapeutic gains. However, some clients choose monthly 'booster sessions' or a single session approach when needed. I will adapt to your therapeutic needs and goals.

How many sessions will I need?

  • This will very much depend on why you are seeking therapy and your goals for therapy which we will agree at the start of the therapeutic process. We will regularly review your progress and collaboratively agree on the right number of sessions and when it would be appropriate to end the therapy.
